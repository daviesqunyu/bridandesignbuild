🏢 De Vries Africa: Office Fit-Out, Westlands

Location:
Westlands, Nairobi
Status:
✅ Completed 2025
Scope:
Design + Build | Project Management
Team Lead:
Diana Situma

🔍 Project Overview

De Vries Africa approached Bridan Design Build to transform an empty shell into a premium-grade, branded office experience. The result: a sleek, high-performance commercial workspace that blends functionality, hospitality-inspired aesthetics, and modern tech-readiness — tailored for a dynamic, international-facing team.

📐 What We Did

  • Full concept-to-execution office interior fit-out
  • Custom bar/lounge area with branded ambiance
  • Modular furniture layout, boardroom partitioning, and work zone planning
  • MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) reconfigurations
  • End-to-end project coordination and subcontractor management

🧱 Key Features

  • Signature bar-lounge area for staff and client engagement
  • Smart lighting, suspended ceiling panels, and acoustic finishes
  • Built-in kitchenette with upgraded plumbing and cabinetry
  • Private meeting zones, reception, and hot-desk capacity
  • 3D renders + actual build aligned to perfection

🎯 Client Benefit

Bridan's agile, design-led construction approach ensured:

  • Seamless coordination between design vision and physical execution
  • Cost-controlled material sourcing without compromising on style
  • Rapid delivery timelines with zero disruption to neighboring tenants
  • A fully branded space that reflects the De Vries identity
